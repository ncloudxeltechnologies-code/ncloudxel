Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

At ncloudxel TECHNOLOGIES, we are committed to protecting your privacy and safeguarding the information you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you interact with our website, services, and communications.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, company name.
  • Technical Information: IP address, browser type, device information.
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent on the website, interaction data.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to inquiries and provide requested services

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance functionality, analyze traffic, and understand user behavior. You may disable cookies through your browser settings; however, some features of the website may not function optimally.

We may use trusted third-party services such as analytics providers or communication tools. These third parties operate under their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard technical and organizational measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. While we strive to protect your data, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure.

We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy or as required by applicable laws.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.

8. Contact Us
